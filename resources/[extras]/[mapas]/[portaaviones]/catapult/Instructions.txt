
Installation:
Drag the Catapult Folder into your Resources
Make sure to update your server.cfg
IMPORTANT - Make sure to install -  https://forum.cfx.re/t/release-v2-fix-holes-in-the-map-up-to-after-hours/25240 (This adds the Aircraft Carrier)

To only allow custom planes, edit the `planeList` array to add your airplanes (all caps)
